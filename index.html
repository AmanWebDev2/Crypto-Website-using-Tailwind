    What is Metaacres?

    World's first Mixed Reality Metaverse Real Estate marketplace, making your property selling, buying experience more futuristic.

    Mixed Reality Meaning - Mixed reality is a blend of physical and digital worlds, unlocking natural and intuitive 3D human, computer, and environmental interactions. This new reality is based on advancements in computer vision, graphical processing, display technologies, input systems, and cloud computing.

    About MARET Coin

    MARET is the Binance Smart Chain based Token. The token smart contract charges 3.5% fee on every transaction. This 3.5%fees will be used to promote and development of Metaacres platform.

    Metaacres team will also introduce new platforms that gives a chance to community to earn more MARET.

    MARET is also an utility token, user can advertise on Metaacres Platform or promote their listing, by using MARET token, selected cryptocurrency or bank payment.
